## 🌟 About

Safarbnb is a full-stack web platform inspired by Airbnb, specifically designed for the Lebanese market. We connect travelers with local hosts offering accommodations and authentic experiences, from Beirut's vibrant neighborhoods to Tripoli's historic streets, and from Byblos' ancient ruins to the snowy peaks of Faraya.



## ✨ Features

### For Travelers
- 🏠 **Hotel & Accommodation Booking** - Browse and book hotels, apartments, guesthouses, and unique stays
- 🎯 **Activity Reservations** - Book local tours, experiences, and activities
- 🔍 **Advanced Search & Filters** - Filter by location, price, amenities, ratings, and dates
-  **Interactive Maps** - Explore properties and activities on integrated maps
- ⭐ **Reviews & Ratings** - Read authentic reviews from other travelers
- 💳 **Secure Payments** - Safe and encrypted payment processing
- 📱 **Responsive Design** - Seamless experience on desktop, tablet, and mobile

### For Hosts
- 📝 **Easy Listing Creation** - Add properties or activities with rich descriptions and photos
- 📊 **Dashboard & Analytics** - Track bookings, revenue, and performance
- 🗓️ **Calendar Management** - Manage availability and bookings
- 💬 **Messaging System** - Communicate directly with guests
- 💰 **Payment Processing** - Receive payments securely
