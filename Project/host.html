Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get paid as a host?

We process payments 24 hours after your guest checks in. You can receive payments via direct deposit, PayPal, or other available methods in your country.

How much does it cost to list on BookEase?

It's completely free to list your property or activity on BookEase. We only take a small commission (3%) when you get a booking.

How long does the review process take?

Most listings are approved within 24-48 hours. We review all submissions to ensure they meet our quality and safety standards.

Can I list multiple properties or activities?

Yes, you can list as many properties or activities as you want under your host account. Each one will be reviewed separately.

What happens if my property gets damaged?

BookEase offers a Host Protection Program that covers up to $1 million in damages. You can submit a claim within 14 days of the guest's checkout.
